Who We Are

TCKid is a active global community of Third Culture Kid (TCK) adults and youth across geographical boundaries. Read about our mission and vision, and our official partners and staff.

  • Open to all ages, races, cultures, and backgrounds.
  • Over 50 Local Groups worldwide in Europe, North America, Middle East, & Asia-Pacific.
  • Read 127+ Stories & Blogs from TCKs
  • Access TCK Resources, blogs, research, groups, poetry, music, book reviews...
  • Watch Videos and documentaries by TCKs
  • Run by volunteers & completely free to join.
  • TCKid helps you connect with over 50 Local Groups Worldwide
